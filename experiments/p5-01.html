Geodata Weaving Map Selector

A first p5.js assignment inspired by Zach Lieberman's dense generative language. Choose a place, drag across the field, and use the mouse wheel to tighten or loosen the woven map texture.

Map Selector

Each button swaps the underlying street-grid rhythm, palette pressure, and strand density. A faint generated map sits underneath so the weave still feels shaped by geography.

Assignment prompt

Design a map selector that treats place as texture: streets become threads, landmarks become blocks of color, and movement becomes a filter.
