Dropout Garden

A procedural point cloud built to fail on purpose. The patio and bench resolve into clean, gridded geometry, the kind of surface a scanner (and a real-estate listing) can price. The plants never fully resolve: canopy points thin and fade with the scanner, blossoms only exist in season, and whole branch tips can go missing. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom.
