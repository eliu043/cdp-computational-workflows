The Panopticon Skybox

A cube-map room built from six real, situated images of institutional vision, keyed to London's surveillance camera culture: a congestion-charge CCTV mast, a Sentinel-2 satellite pass, a street-level gridlock view, a thermal infrared storefront, a LiDAR point cloud, and a photogrammetry city model. Drag to look around from inside the cube. A reflective lens at the center of the room shows all six feeds stitched across a single curved surface at once.
